Group splitting

Split any bill, exactly how you want.

Equal, by percentage, or exact amounts — or scan the receipt and tap who had what. Smart settle-up nets everyone out to the fewest payments possible.

  • No sign-up for friends — add anyone to a bill, even without the app
  • Exclude anyone who wasn't part of the bill
  • Settle up in a tap — no awkward reminders
  • Real-time sync — everyone sees new expenses instantly
  • Check group balances at a glance from a Home Screen widget
AI receipt scanning

Snap the receipt. AI does the rest.

Point your camera at a receipt and AI reads every line item — then you just tap who had what.

  • AI itemizes every line — no manual entry
  • Assign each item to whoever ordered it — guests welcome, no app needed
  • Share the breakdown via iMessage, QR, or AirDrop
Down to the last item

You had one beer. Don't pay for five.

When the table orders a round, most apps split it evenly or leave you doing the math. Node Expense lets you assign exact quantities — John had three, you had one — so everyone pays for exactly what they had.

  • Assign quantities per person — not just who touched the item
  • Tax and tip follow each person's real share, to the penny
  • The total always adds up — no missing or double-counted items
Group comments

Talk it out — right on the expense.

“Wait, what was this charge?” Most split apps send that conversation off to a separate group chat. Node Expense puts a comment thread on every shared expense, so you can ask, explain, and agree right where the money is.

  • A comment thread on every group expense
  • Push alerts the moment someone replies
  • See which expenses have a conversation at a glance
On your own

Not splitting? Just track it solo.

Node Expense isn't only for groups. Log your own spending in seconds — scan a receipt or tap it in — sorted into your own categories, with budgets and insights working quietly in the background. No account, and your personal data never leaves your device.

  • Scan to auto-fill the merchant, total, date, and category
  • Your own categories, with emoji and budgets
  • Add an expense in one tap — straight from a Control Center widget
  • A monthly overview card shows your total spend and how it compares to last month
  • A budget pace ring shows how much of your monthly budget is left, at a glance
